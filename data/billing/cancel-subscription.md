# Cancel Subscription

## 1. What It Does
If you no longer need Pro features, you can cancel your subscription at any time.

## 2. Cancellation Process & Access
- **Immediate Effects**: When you click **"Cancel Subscription"**, the subscription is **not cancelled immediately**. It remains active until the end of the current billing cycle.
- **User Access**: You retain full access to all paid features and services until the end of the current billing cycle.

## 3. Undoing the Cancellation
- **Reverting**: If you cancel your subscription and want to undo it before the current billing cycle ends, you can easily reverse the cancellation.
- **How to Undo**:
  1. Navigate to **Payment & Billing** settings.
  2. Click the **"Revert Cancellation"** button to resume active billing.

## 4. Common Questions

**Q: What happens to my access immediately after clicking "Cancel Subscription", and how can it be undone?**  
A: Your subscription remains active and you retain full access to all features until the end of the current billing cycle. You can undo the cancellation by going to **Payment & Billing** settings and clicking **"Revert Cancellation"** before the cycle ends.
