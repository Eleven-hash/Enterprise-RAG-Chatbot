# Wallet Credits

## 1. What It Does
Wallet Credits allow Pro and Free users to pay-as-you-go for advanced AI Copilot services after reaching their monthly plans' quotas.

## 2. Standard Credit Top-Up Packages
The following standard credit top-up packages are available:
- **£10** for **100 credits**
- **£25** for **250 credits**
- **£50** for **500 credits**
- **£100** for **1000 credits**
- **£200** for **2000 credits**

All pricing packages automatically include VAT in the payment.

## 3. Cost of AI Services
- Standard AI Copilot Query: **1 credit**
- Deep Project Analysis Report: **5 credits**

## 4. Refund Policy
- **Unused Credits**: If you have unused credits in your wallet, you can request a refund for these credits.
- **Refund Process**: To initiate a refund, contact billing support at billing@enterprise-rag.com.
- **Conditions**: Refunds are subject to the terms of service, meaning they must be requested within 30 days of purchase and the credits must be fully unused.

## 5. Common Questions

**Q: What are the standard credit top-up packages available in the Wallet, and is VAT included?**  
A: The packages are £10 (100 credits), £25 (250 credits), £50 (500 credits), £100 (1000 credits), and £200 (2000 credits). **VAT is included** in the payment.

**Q: What is the refund policy for unused credits?**  
A: Unused credits are refundable by contacting Billing Support at billing@enterprise-rag.com within 30 days of purchase.

**Q: What is the cost difference between standard and deep AI analysis?**  
A: Standard queries cost **1 credit**, whereas deep project analyses cost **5 credits**.
