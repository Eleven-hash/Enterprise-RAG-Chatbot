# Pricing Plans

## 1. What It Does
Enterprise RAG offers flexible pricing plans to accommodate teams of all sizes, from individual developers to global enterprises.

## 2. Subscription Tiers
1. **Free Tier**: Up to 5 users, basic task boards, and standard integrations.
2. **Pro Tier** (£12/month per user): Unlimited boards, advanced integrations, and full AI Copilot access.
3. **Enterprise Tier** (Custom Pricing): SSO, dedicated support, custom data retention policies, and custom SLAs.

## 3. VAT Information
- **VAT Policy**: All subscription prices and credit top-ups automatically **include VAT** in the shown payment amount. There are no hidden fees.
