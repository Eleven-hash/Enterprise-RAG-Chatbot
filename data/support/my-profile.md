# My Profile

## 1. What It Does
The My Profile page allows you to manage your personal account settings, notification preferences, and password.

## 2. Profile Details
- Username
- Avatar
- Timezone
- Password Update

## 3. Email Change Policy
- **Security Policy**: For account security, the registered email address cannot be changed directly from your profile settings page.
- **How to Change Registered Email**:
  1. Contact Support by emailing support@enterprise-rag.com.
  2. Provide identification verification as requested by the help desk.
  3. The support team will update your email address in the database after verification.

## 4. Common Questions

**Q: How do I change the registered email address of my profile?**  
A: You cannot change it directly in the profile settings. To change your registered email, contact customer support at support@enterprise-rag.com.
