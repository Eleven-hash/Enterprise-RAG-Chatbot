# Time Tracker

## 1. What It Does
Time Tracker allows team members to log working hours against tasks. This provides project managers with real-time visibility into the effort spent on individual project segments, helping with budget tracking and resource allocation.

## 2. Where to Find It
- Navigate to: **Sidebar → Productivity Tools → Time Tracker**
- Direct route: `/app/time-tracker`

## 3. How to Use Time Tracker
1. Click the **Start Timer** button when starting a task, or log hours manually using the entry form.
2. Select the task from the dropdown menu to link the logged hours.
3. Review your weekly or monthly timesheet overview.
4. Export timesheets as a CSV or PDF report.

## 4. Common Questions

**Q: Can I log hours for tasks assigned to other team members?**  
A: No. By default, users can only log hours on tasks that are assigned to them or where they are listed as a collaborator.
