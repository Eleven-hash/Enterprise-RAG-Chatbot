# 📝 RAG Chatbot Accuracy Test Results

This file compiles the questions asked and answers generated during the testing session for the **Enterprise RAG** RAG chatbot.

## ❓ Question 1: What AI tools are available?

### 🤖 Chatbot Response:
There are two main AI tools available:

### 1. **AI Copilot (Task Assistant)**
- **Purpose**: This tool is designed to **analyze tasks**, **explain task blockages**, and **answer project questions**.
- **Key Features**:
  - Auto-generates task descriptions.
  - Summarizes long comments or message threads.
  - Helps clear up misalignments on active tasks.
- **Access**: You can use this tool by visiting [AI Copilot](https://enterprise-rag.com/app/ai-copilot) or via the task card panel.

### 2. **AI Bottleneck Analysis**
- **Purpose**: This tool scans your workspace and **identifies tasks blocking team progress**.
- **Key Features**:
  - Monitors task durations and comments.
  - Flags delayed tasks automatically.
- **Access**: Accessible directly from the Project Board Analytics view.

## ❓ Question 2: What are the task priority levels supported by the Task Manager?

### 🤖 Chatbot Response:
The **Task Manager** supports the following four task priority levels:

- **Low**: For minor tasks with minimal urgency or impact on project deadlines.
- **Medium**: For standard tasks that need to be resolved during normal sprint schedules.
- **High**: For important tasks that affect milestone progress and should be prioritized.
- **Critical**: For blocker tasks that stall project timelines or require immediate attention.

## ❓ Question 4: What fields are shown on each task card in the Task Board?

### 🤖 Chatbot Response:
Each task card on the **Task Board** displays the following details:

- **Task Name**: The title/description of the task.
- **Assignee**: The team member responsible for the task.

### 🤖 Chatbot Response:
On the **Task Board**, the specific toggle filter available is:

### **Hide Completed Tasks**
- **Function**: This toggle allows users to **exclude completed tasks** from the active board view.

## ❓ Question 6: What are the four quick actions available directly from a task card?

### 🤖 Chatbot Response:
From a **task card**, you can perform the following four quick actions directly:

1. **Assign Task**: Assign the task to a team member or update the assignee.
2. **Change Status**: Move the task to a different status column (e.g., from `In Progress` to `Under Review`).
3. **Add Subtask**: Create nested subtasks to break down the primary task.
4. **Archive**: Remove the task from the active board and send it to archive storage.

## ❓ Question 7: What is a "Bottleneck" and how does Enterprise RAG identify a Bottleneck task?

### 🤖 Chatbot Response:
A **Bottleneck** is a task that blocks team progress and stalls the project timeline.

### How Enterprise RAG Identifies a Bottleneck Task:
- **Duration in Review**: The task remains in the `Under Review` status column for more than **48 hours** without any updates.
- **Overdue with Active Discussion**: The task is past its **due date** and has more than **5 unresolved comments**, indicating confusion or blockers among the assignees.

## ❓ Question 9: What happens to a subscription and user access immediately after clicking "Cancel Subscription", and how can it be undone?

### 🤖 Chatbot Response:
When you click **"Cancel Subscription"**, the following occurs:

### Immediate Effects:
- **Subscription Status**: The subscription is **not cancelled immediately**. It remains active until the end of the current billing cycle.
- **User Access**: You retain full access to all paid features and services until the end of that billing cycle.

### Undoing the Cancellation:
- If you change your mind before the current billing cycle ends, you can easily reverse the cancellation:
  - Navigate to the **Payment & Billing** settings.
  - Click **"Revert Cancellation"** to keep your subscription active and resume automatic renewals.

## ❓ Question 10: What are three examples of "Bottleneck Highlights" that Enterprise RAG displays to explain why a task is blocked?

### 🤖 Chatbot Response:
Enterprise RAG displays the following three color-coded **Bottleneck Highlights** on the dashboard to explain blockages:

1. **Stuck in Review**: The task is stuck waiting for approval or QA feedback for over 48 hours.
2. **Missed Deadline**: The task's due date has passed without its status changing to Completed.
3. **High Comment Volume**: The task has a large volume of active comments, indicating confusion or debate among assignees.
